Push - Day 1
Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
  • Incline DB Bench
    Incline DB Bench
    Set bench to 30-45¬∞. Lie back with dumbbells at chest level. Press up and slightly together at the top. Lower slowly with control. Targets upper chest.
  • Cable Lat Raise
    Cable Lat Raise
    Stand sideways to cable machine. Grab handle with far hand. Raise arm out to side until parallel to ground. Control the descent. Builds shoulder width.
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions
    Overhead Tricep Extensions
    Hold dumbbell/cable overhead with both hands. Lower behind head by bending elbows. Extend arms back up. Keep elbows tight. Stretches triceps fully.
  • Machine Chest Flies
    Machine Chest Flies
    Sit with back flat against pad. Bring handles together in front of chest. Squeeze at the center. Control the stretch. Isolates chest muscles.
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press or Machine
    Shoulder Press
    Press dumbbells or machine handles overhead from shoulder level. Fully extend arms at top. Lower with control. Builds overall shoulder strength.
  • Cable Tricep Pushdowns
    Cable Tricep Pushdowns
    Stand facing cable machine. Push bar/rope down until arms fully extended. Keep elbows stationary at sides. Slow return. Targets tricep definition.
‚ö° Finishers
  • Bench Press
    Bench Press
    Lie on flat bench. Lower bar to chest. Press up explosively. Keep feet planted. The king of chest exercises.
  • Dips
    Dips
    Support yourself on parallel bars. Lower body by bending arms. Press back up. Lean forward for chest, upright for triceps.
  • Push-ups
    Push-ups
    Start in plank position. Lower chest to ground. Push back up. Keep core tight. Classic bodyweight chest builder.
Pull - Day 2
Back, Biceps
  • Seated Cable Rows
    Seated Cable Rows
    Sit upright, pull handle to torso. Squeeze shoulder blades together. Keep chest up. Slowly extend arms back. Builds back thickness.
  • Preacher Curls
    Preacher Curls
    Rest arms on preacher bench pad. Curl weight up, squeezing biceps at top. Lower slowly with control. Isolates biceps peak.
  • Lat Pulldowns
    Lat Pulldowns
    Pull bar down to upper chest. Keep torso upright. Squeeze lats at bottom. Control the weight up. Builds back width.
  • Cable Curls
    Cable Curls
    Stand facing cable machine. Curl bar up while keeping elbows stationary. Squeeze at top. Constant tension on biceps.
  • Chest Supported Row
    Chest Supported Row
    Lie face down on incline bench. Pull dumbbells/handles to sides. Squeeze shoulder blades. Removes lower back strain.
  • Face Pulls
    Face Pulls
    Pull rope attachment to face level. Spread handles apart at your ears. Focus on rear delts. Great for posture and shoulder health.
  • Shrugs
    Shrugs
    Hold dumbbells or barbell at sides. Shrug shoulders straight up toward ears. Squeeze traps at top. Lower with control. Builds massive traps.
‚ö° Finishers
  • Pull-ups
    Pull-ups
    Hang from bar, pull chin over bar. Lower with control. Use full range of motion. Ultimate back builder.
  • DB Rows
    DB Rows
    Bend over with hand on bench for support. Pull dumbbell to hip. Keep back flat. Squeeze at top. Unilateral back work.
  • Hammer Curls w/ Rope
    Hammer Curls w/ Rope
    Hold rope attachment with neutral grip. Curl up keeping palms facing each other. Targets brachialis and forearms.

Legs - Day 4
Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes
  • Seated Hamstring Curl
    Seated Hamstring Curl
    Sit in machine, curl legs back. Squeeze hamstrings at contraction. Control the weight down. Isolates hamstring development.
  • Back Squat
    Back Squat
    Bar on upper back. Squat down until thighs parallel. Drive up through heels. Keep chest up. The king of leg exercises.
  • Leg Extension
    Leg Extension
    Sit in machine, extend legs until straight. Squeeze quads at top. Lower with control. Isolates quadriceps.
  • Hip Abduction
    Hip Abduction
    Sit in machine, push legs apart against resistance. Squeeze glutes at end. Control return. Targets outer glutes and hips.
  • DB Romanian Deadlift
    DB Romanian Deadlift
    Hold dumbbells, hinge at hips keeping back flat. Feel stretch in hamstrings. Drive hips forward to stand. Hamstring and glute builder.
  • Calf Raises
    Calf Raises
    Stand on edge of platform with heels hanging off. Rise up on toes as high as possible. Squeeze calves at top. Lower slowly below platform level. Builds calf size and strength.

Daily Flexibility, Mobility & Nutrition

Stretching
  • Hip Flexor Stretch
    Hip Flexor Stretch
    Lunge position, push hips forward. Feel stretch in front of hip. Hold 30-60 seconds each side. Critical for mobility.
  • Inner Thigh Stretch
    Inner Thigh Stretch
    Wide stance, shift weight side to side. Feel stretch in inner thighs. Opens up hips and improves mobility.
  • Hamstring Stretch (one foot at time)
    Hamstring Stretch
    Extend one leg, hinge at hips. Reach toward toes. Hold 30-60 seconds each leg. Essential for lower body flexibility.
  • Middle Splits
    Middle Splits
    Slide legs apart sideways as far as comfortable. Hold and breathe. Progressive flexibility work. Be patient.
  • Front Splits
    Front Splits
    One leg forward, one back. Lower down as far as comfortable. Hold and breathe. Work both sides. Takes time.
