Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

    Incline DB Bench
    Set bench to 30-45Â°. Lie back with dumbbells at chest level. Press up and slightly together at the top. Lower slowly with control. Targets upper chest.
  • Cable Lat Raise
    Cable Lat Raise
    Stand sideways to cable machine. Grab handle with far hand. Raise arm out to side until parallel to ground. Control the descent. Builds shoulder width.
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions
    Overhead Tricep Extensions
    Hold dumbbell/cable overhead with both hands. Lower behind head by bending elbows. Extend arms back up. Keep elbows tight. Stretches triceps fully.
  • Machine Chest Flies
    Machine Chest Flies
    Sit with back flat against pad. Bring handles together in front of chest. Squeeze at the center. Control the stretch. Isolates chest muscles.
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press or Machine
    Shoulder Press
    Press dumbbells or machine handles overhead from shoulder level. Fully extend arms at top. Lower with control. Builds overall shoulder strength.
  • Cable Tricep Pushdowns
    Cable Tricep Pushdowns
    Stand facing cable machine. Push bar/rope down until arms fully extended. Keep elbows stationary at sides. Slow return. Targets tricep definition.

    Dips

  • Seated Cable Rows
    Seated Cable Rows
    Sit upright, pull handle to torso. Squeeze shoulder blades together. Keep chest up. Slowly extend arms back. Builds back thickness.
  • Preacher Curls
    Preacher Curls
    Rest arms on preacher bench pad. Curl weight up, squeezing biceps at top. Lower slowly with control. Isolates biceps peak.
  • Lat Pulldowns
    Lat Pulldowns
    Pull bar down to upper chest. Keep torso upright. Squeeze lats at bottom. Control the weight up. Builds back width.
  • Cable Curls
    Cable Curls
    Stand facing cable machine. Curl bar up while keeping elbows stationary. Squeeze at top. Constant tension on biceps.
  • Chest Supported Row
    Chest Supported Row
    Lie face down on incline bench. Pull dumbbells/handles to sides. Squeeze shoulder blades. Removes lower back strain.

    Pull-ups

    DB Rows

    Hammer Curls w/ Rope

    Seated Hamstring Curl

    Back Squat
    Bar on upper back. Squat down until thighs parallel. Drive up through heels. Keep chest up. The king of leg exercises.
  • Leg Extension
    Leg Extension
    Sit in machine, extend legs until straight. Squeeze quads at top. Lower with control. Isolates quadriceps.
  • Hip Abduction
    Hip Abduction
    Sit in machine, push legs apart against resistance. Squeeze glutes at end. Control return. Targets outer glutes and hips.
  • DB Romanian Deadlift
    DB Romanian Deadlift
    Hold dumbbells, hinge at hips keeping back flat. Feel stretch in hamstrings. Drive hips forward to stand. Hamstring and glute builder.
